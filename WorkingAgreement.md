Working Agreement 
Members: Alexis, Julio, Norma, Trayia

Culture 
Trust
Allow everyone to keep an open-minded to speak their ideas, opinions, and decisions to have quality in our group.
Openly sharing information, and asking for help when needed.
Do the work you’d say to do, be consistent and respect other’s work and time.
Own up to your mistakes and truths.
Conflict 
Conflict needs to be resolved in a peaceful and respectful manner.
Conflict is inevitable and it is on us as a team to find a common ground and maintain professionalism.
If initiating a heated debate, focus on the issue instead of attacking the person, stay calm, and acknowledge their emotion.
Understanding different perspectives and respecting other’s needs.
Commitment 
It does not have to be a daily meet up. 
Plan out 2-3 hours to meet a week whether physical or via teams.
Come to class as much as possible. We all have personal things that may hinder us from being here. Try to communicate if that is the case at least 24 hours in advance.
Consistent missing class or failure to communicate why will result in member drops.

Meetings
Meeting times will take on Mondays & Wednesdays after 12:00pm - 4:30pm and Tuesdays & Thursdays after 10:00am.
Meetings time will take place on teams to the time stated above.
Alexis will take notes in meetings, and notes will be stored on GitHub.
The team will have reminders on a card or device written down the time and place.
Let the team know at least 24 hours in advance if not possible to make the meeting.
If missing meetings, they will be dropped.

Communication
Communicate any proposed changes at least 24 hours in advance or call for a team meeting for feedback and approval.
If you have to miss class and are well enough to listen in, call another team member on teams so you don’t miss important information. (Do not abuse this)
Proposed changes or submissions feedback can be found under announcements for the group in Canvas.
If you need immediate feedback on teams or directly on canvas. Also make sure you are checking your messages on both platforms.


Decisions
Try to make most decisions as a collective.
No one person is the leader, we all have the ability to lead, the focus is collaboration.
Present ideas, details, or final feedback on canvas as an announcement or in person.
If a majority of members are missing, come to an agreement on final decision making criteria so it aligns with all values as much as possible.


Development
Follow agile software development methods.
Take it slow to go fast.
It's ok not to get it right the first time the goal is maintainable code.
Ask for help. Yes we are all hyperintelligent but we will all need help eventually.
Pay attention to the details.
Incorrectly done or submitted work results in a lesser grade for all of us even in the event we fix it. 


Workflow
The task board will be used to evenly delegate all work.
Members will follow their task flow and ask for help in an event they are stuck in.
Issues will be created immediately if one arises and will be posted on git hub and an announcement for said issue will be posted on canvas.
Issues get moved when they are resolved and no sooner.
Anyone can create an issue as long as it is a reasonable one that can not be solved without proper team feedback.
The team member who created the issue will move it once it is confirmed it is resolved.
Procedure: create issue, work issue, confirm issue is resolved, and move it to closed.
